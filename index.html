FLEET MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


MyVeego is the fleet management system platform. We help business by managing your fleets with real-time visibility of vehicle location, status, driver behavior, diagnostics and in-depth analytics of the data.

OUR SERVICES

We provides four services type to satisfy various needs efficiently.

Dashboard as a Service

Help to accomplish end to end tasks relating to vehicles fleet from acquisition to disposal.

Hardware as a Service

OPEX-based, fully managed tracker devices to capture real-time location and vehicle information.

API as a Service

An Application Programming Interface (API) enabling customers and third-party developers to effortlessly consume location, status, driver and diagnostics data from other systems.

Analytics as a Service

Our analytics service provide fleet owners with a powerful tool to increase billing efficiency and reduce costs.
